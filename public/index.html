Solar Prioritization Tool

California Center for Sustainable Communities at UCLA

This is an interactive tool for prioritizing the location of Advanced Energy Communities in Los Angeles (LA) County. The tool has a particular focus on Disadvantaged Communities, which often have fewer resources installing energy efficiency or renewable energy, along with less robust grid infrastructure. The tool will be a resource for LA County communities as they look to expand innovative designs for future communities. It will also serve as a methodological roadmap for such analyses across the state.
